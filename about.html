About Us

Introduction

Mission Statement

At Packaged Hearts, our mission is to provide essential care packages to homeless populations across the West Coast. We aim to cultivate a spirit of empathy and generosity, advocating for greater awareness and care for those in need.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to establish chapters nationwide, creating opportunities for communities to support their local homeless populations directly and effectively.

Our Story

Founding History

Packaged Hearts was founded in November 2023 by Varsha Jagadeesh as a small family initiative. Beginning with local fundraisers and distributions in Fresno, San Francisco, and Oakland, we have since expanded across the West Coast. Our journey continues as we formalize our organization to offer a reliable platform for community involvement in assisting homeless populations.

Milestones

  • First fundraiser: November 2023
  • First Distribution: December 2023
  • First expansion of chapters: June 2024
  • Establishment as a legal entity: August 2024 (tentative)

Our Impact

Packaged Hearts has grown to include four chapters across the West Coast. Through our efforts, we have raised significant funds and distributed hundreds of care packages, positively impacting many lives.

Our Programs

Care Package Creation

We assemble care packages containing essential items such as toiletries and food, providing crucial support to the homeless community.

Advocacy

Our Instagram pages serve as platforms for raising awareness about homelessness. We highlight the ongoing crises in various towns, urging societal change and support.
